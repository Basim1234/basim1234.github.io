Tailored Blinds

Tailored blinds are custom-made window treatments designed to fit specific window dimensions and meet individual style preferences.

Roman Blinds

Roman blinds are a stylish and elegant window treatment option that adds a touch of sophistication to any room. Unlike roller blinds, Roman blinds are made from soft fabric that folds neatly into pleats when raised.

Roller Blinds

Roller blinds are a popular window treatment choice known for their simplicity and versatility. They consist of a single piece of fabric that rolls up and down using a rolling mechanism, typically operated by a chain or spring.

Vertical Blinds

Vertical blinds are a practical and versatile window treatment option, ideal for large windows and sliding glass doors. They consist of individual slats, or vanes, that hang vertically from a track and can be tilted open or closed, as well as drawn to the side, for precise light control and privacy.
